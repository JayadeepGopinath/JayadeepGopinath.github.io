About

I build systems that are honest about what they can and can't do.

A student engineer based in Chicago, targeting Fall 2026 SWE and AI internships.

I'm a Master's student in Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology, graduating May 2027, drawn to the intersection of backend systems and AI infrastructure. My work sits at the layer where model outputs meet production reliability. That means RAG pipelines, agentic loops, MCP servers, and the observability that makes any of it trustworthy.

The projects on this page share one habit. I measure before I claim. Notification-System-Go was load-tested with k6 to 170 requests per second, and the README documents where it breaks. Agentic-RAG-MCP has a RAGAS-style eval pipeline because "does my RAG work?" should be a testable question, not a hope.

Before the flagship push, my undergraduate project Naari Shakthi, an AI-powered rural healthcare dispensary, earned recognition from the Government of Karnataka (KSCST) and became an IJTRE Xplore publication. Engineering that reaches actual users, with measurement to prove it.

Right now I'm looking for a Fall 2026 SWE or AI Engineering internship, preferably at an MCP-native or AI-infrastructure company. F-1 CPT eligible. No H-1B sponsorship required for the internship.
